About Us

Maine Recovery Services, Inc. specializes in assisting health care professionals in attaining the highest degree of customer service possible. We accomplish excellence in service by working closely with your staff, and by focusing on the needs of providers who are facing the difficulties of delivering health care in today's constantly changing environment. At Maine Recovery Services, Inc., we know the complexities facing all providers in delivering the most cost effective care without compromising quality and service to the patient

Our clients enjoy pleasant differences from our competitors in the region. First and foremost, we are a result-oriented company. Together we work within the existing internal office structure to define goals and to obtain measurable solutions that are quantifiable and real. Our approach begins with the attitude that our clients are best at determining where their specific needs are. We strive to accommodate those needs until our clients' priorities and our priorities are one and the same. You can be assured that we give professional personalized service to all of our customers. Our rates are competitive and negotiated with each provider. Maine Recovery Services, Inc. is committed to building our reputation for excellence one customer at a time. Maine Recovery Services, Inc. will be there to help you with the special projects and daily challenges that never seem to diminish. We hope you will give us the opportunity to serve you in the future by leading our expertise and assistance to your health care business concerns. We are confident that together we can help your facility overcome financial obstacles in this tough exciting industry.
